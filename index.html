My name is Muhammet Alper Sayılır, and I was born on May 24, 2002, in Gaziantep, Turkey. I completed my high school education at Gülşen Batar Anatolian High School graduating with honors in 2019. In 2020, I began my English-taught Computer Engineering studies at Hasan Kalyoncu University with a full scholarship. Throughout my university years, I was an active student, both as a member and a core team member in various clubs, including the Erasmus Student Network HKU, Game Factory HKU, and HKU Sports Clubs. During my university education, I developed my skills in Python, C, C++, C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Oracle SQL, Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender, Arduino, and robotics. I had the opportunity to participate in the Erasmus+ exchange program at the University of Ostrava in the Czech Republic in both 2022 and 2024, which significantly enhanced my problem-solving abilities and allowed me to work effectively with multicultural teams, ultimately broadening my horizons.
